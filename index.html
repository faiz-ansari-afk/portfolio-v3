FakeOffice

A real office. For a fake workday. With real people.

Remote work is great — until it isn't. After months of WFH, the walls start closing in. FakeOffice is a drop-in coworking concept designed for people who just need to get out of the house for a day — without the commitment of a permanent desk or membership.

🏢
Show up. Work. Leave.
Book a spot for a day — no subscriptions, no long-term contracts. Just a desk, good wifi, and coffee.
🤝
Collaborate with strangers
Every day is a new mix of engineers, designers, writers, and founders. Share ideas, get unstuck, make connections.
🧠
Built for tech enthusiasts
Curated for people who think deeply and build things. Optional evening sessions for demos, talks, and side-project pitches.
🏠
Escape the home trap
WFH blurs work and rest. FakeOffice gives you the mental separation — a "third place" that's neither home nor a traditional office.
